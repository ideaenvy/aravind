DO, DNB
Designation
Director - IT & Systems, Aravind Eye Care System
Cheif Medical Officer
Cheif, Retina Vitreous Services
Location
Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai
Introduction Research & Publications Activities Awards & Recognition

Dr. Kim joined as a Medical Consultant in Vitreous Retina and Services at Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai and he is head of Vitreo Retina Department and Program Director, Aravind Telemedicine Network in the year 2000. At present he is the Chief Medical Officer of Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai.

He has published several research papers in peer reviewed International Journals, State Journals; 3 Publications about Teleophthalmology and has authored 2 book chapters so far. He has participated in various National and International meetings in Ophthalmology.
