Insurance

We will help you in fulfilling the formalities and necessary paper work for availing the Insurance Services. The insurance coverage is valid only for inpatient procedures (48 hours hospitalisation) and approval from the insurance provider is required. A deposit is required during admission at the in-patient billing department. The deposit amount will be refunded once your insurance provider authorises the treatment payment. In the case insurance coverage is not provided within 48 hours of admission, you must settle the hospital bill and claim reimbursement from your insurance provider.

Aravind Eye Hospital accepts medical policies from 36 different insurance companies (including State & Central Government and Corporate companies) and 15 different Third Party Administrators (TPA's) across India. The list will change from time to time and kindly contact Insurance Department Staffs for an updated list.

Cashless Do's & Dont's

Hospital TPA/Insurance Department is only a facilitator for communication between patient and insurance companies. It does not approve or deny claims and is not responsible for delay in approvals or denials from TPA/Insurance companies

Cashless request is considered only if following documents submitted within 12 hrs of hospitalisation as required by insurance companies. Otherwise cash payment will be required.

Submission of Cashless request form is not admission of pay ability under insurance and cashless can be denied by insurance / TPA if treatment is not covered under applicable terms & conditions applicable to your insurance policy

In case of denials or partial approvals, patient / relative has to make full balance payment in cash before discharge or planned surgical procedure.

Certain expenses are not payable under terms and conditions of insurance policy for which deposit in advance has to be made at time of admission.

You need to fill the claim form and sign it at relevant places before discharge along with the final credit bill.

Final bill once prepared is sent to insurance company / TPA for final approval along with medical documentation. The insurance company / TPA can deny earlier approved services during discharge and patient will have to pay the full bill in cash before discharge in such cases.

If Patient / Relative does not wish to wait for final approval from TPA/Insurance company, the patient / relative will have to pay the bill in cash and seek reimbursement later.
