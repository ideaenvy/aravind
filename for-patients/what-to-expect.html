At Aravind Eye Hospitals great stress is placed on maximum utilisation of resources. Two-third of the outpatient visits and three-fourth of the surgeries were serviced to the poor either free or at a steeply subsidized rate.

Working Hours :Aravind Eye Hospitals are open Monday – Saturday, 7:00 am to 6 pm

Note : All Sundays are holidays for outpatients except emergency cases. We are always open for emergencies.

For referral patients :The patient has to bring his recent medical reports along with the referral letter. After registration, he or she will be taken to the appropriate specialty clinic.

For second opinion :Attach all the recent medical reports and clinic photos in Expert Opinions. Please detail the eye problem. Our team will respond to you with the possible treatment options, length of stay (if needed) & cost of treatment.

For Visitors :The hospital solicits cooperation for maintaining a quiet ambience. Children are strictly not allowed to visit or stay with the patient

Security Instructions :You are advised not to bring valuables to the hospital. Do not hand money or valuables to strangers. The ward nurse on each floor can be approached for more details. Closed circuit camera is provided in the ward for the patient’s safety. For more assistance, contact the Manager in Patient Services, who is available on the ground floor from 7:30 am to 6 pm.

Conduct with Hospital Staff :During your stay, you will come in contact with any hospital staff, such as doctors, nurses, counselors, housekeepers, and sanitary workers. All of them are working very hard to make your stay at Aravind as comfortable as possible. Please avoid giving tips and gifts to the hospital staff.

A detailed eye examination will be done at one of the general units. Based on the evaluation, the patient may be directed to Speciality Clinics (give link to Speciality clinics page)

There are many different eye conditions; you will need to have a detailed eye examination which could take upto 2 hours, depending on the complexity of tests required. The durations indicated below are an average.

Information to Outpatients

Outpatient waiting area

Outpatient procedure

Patients who require surgery or monitoring will be asked to get admitted in the hospital. Patients can choose from the different types of rooms based on the availability. At the time of counseling, the counselors will assist you in selecting the room, surgical techniques and implants available. Based on your convenience, you can choose the package of your choice. Cashless facilities and reimbursement certificates can be availed as applicable.

Many of the surgeries are being done as day care procedures. Patients undergoing day care procedures need not stay in the hospital after the surgery. The package charges vary based on the type of accommodation selected for procedures other than the day care. Following are the different types of accommodation facilities available:

Suite

A Class(A/C)

A Class(Non A/C)

B Class

C Class

General Ward

Room Reservations :Aravind offers a facility for advance room reservations, following the doctor’s advice to get admitted for surgery or treatment at a later date.

Discharge Procedure :Patients are requested to vacate the room before 10:00 am on the day of discharge. Instructions regarding post-operative care and medications, along with a discharge summary, will be explained by the ophthalmic assistants at the time of discharge.

Billing and Insurance :On admission, a deposit equivalent to or approximate to the treatment or surgery charges will be collected. The balance, if any, will be collected / refunded at the time of discharge based on the surgery/treatment and the type of accommodation chosen. Bills are payable by cash, with insurance claims, if any. Credit cards are accepted and a 2.3% service charge will be collected.

Food :The restaurant is located on the ground floor and is open from 6:30 am to 9 pm. Breakfast, lunch and dinners are available there.

Optical & Medical Shop :The medical shop is located on the ground floor, which functions from 7:30 am to 8 pm on all working days (Monday through Saturday). It is also open on Sundays, from 9 am to 1 pm & 3:30 pm to 5pm.
