About Doctor

Dr Aravind Srinivasan, having worked in the medical field, he had a passion for management. In 2000 he did his MBA with specialization in strategy from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, USA. After MBA, he took over as the Administrator of Aravind Eye Care System and was instrumental in bringing changes to the various facets of the organization. From 2011, he is serving the organization in the role of Director-Projects of Aravind Eye Care System. He is the Board of Govel Trust that runs the Aravind Eye Hospitals. From September 2017, he is leading the Aravind Eye Hospital Chennai as Chief Medical Officer.

On the clinical front, Dr. Aravind is a high volume cataract surgeon. And in Non-Clinical aspects, he is innovating, executing and working with all stake holders in the respective branches to ensure harmony in work, mentoring staff to invent and implement innovations in areas of need to ensure better patient centred care towards.

Key Activities
  • Administration of the hospitals
  • Encourage Innovations
  • Building relationship with partner organizations
  • Evaluation of new projects and ensuring the replication of “ARAVIND Way” through teaching, training and mentoring.
Contributions
  • Guiding B School students from USA and Netherland through projects at AECS – identifying the needs to increase the quality of patient centred care
  • Building Aravind’s New projects and ensuring growth in service care delivery
  • Grooming Managers in the System for increasing operational efficiency
  • As a Trainer and Mentor at Management courses offered by LAICO, he contributes in extending the Aravind Model of high volume, high quality, and affordable cost eye care to developing nation.
Current area of interest
  • Dr. Aravind’s area of specialization concentrates now on overall evaluation and interpretation of performance of each Division and to track progress of performance so as to facilitate benchmarking both internally and externally to achieve patient centered care across the system.
