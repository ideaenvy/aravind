RECOGNITION

  • Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR) recognisation

    Aravind Medical Research Foundation is recognized as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) by the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR).

  • PhD in Biomedical Sciences - Recognized by Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai

    Aravind Medical Research Foundation is recognized as a centre for Ph.D in Biomedical Sciences by Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai, India.

  • PhD affiliation with Alagappa University, Karaikudi

  • PhD affiliation with SASTRA University, Tanjore
