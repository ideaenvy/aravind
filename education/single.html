CME on low vision rehabilitation

May 19 - 20, 2018 Aravind Eye Hospital, Salem Venue : Hotel Radisson, Salem

Register Online Download Brochure

Dear Colleagues,

Greetings!

From Hand-held magnifier to screen readers and smartphones, Low Vision Aids have evolved into great life-changers. Tremendous technological advancements have been seen in this field to make these devices more helpful and at the same time more user friendly, accessible and non-stigmatising. But the topic is not given due importance in many CMEs and Conferences.

To increase the reach and awareness about this multi-dimensional and multi-disciplinary field, we plan an extensive 2 days CME on “Low Vision Services”. We would try to cover the topics from basics to recent advances along with Hands-on training.

We look forward to your presence at the CME.

Warm Regards,
