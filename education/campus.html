CLASSROOMS

All Aravind classrooms are comfortable and well-equipped with advanced teaching technology and enable interaction with the faculty. The classrooms at LAICO allow for flexible layouts, group discussions, case presentations and active interaction. Audience response systems have been introduced to enable participant feedback, opinion gathering and instant training evaluation.

International Trainees

All foreign trainees are required to get a Visa to enter India. Please go through the pre-arrival guidelines that will help you prepare for a hassle-free time here. Foreign trainees may be required to register with the local police upon arrival. See the guidelines to prepare for this. Contact your course coordinator to assist you in this.

Trainee Accommodation

On site accommodation facilities, with catering services, are available within the campus at all our centers. The type of facilities offered are different at each center. PG Resident Quarters are available at all centers for the trainees. Short term trainees to Madurai can opt to stay at Inspiration - the international trainees hostel.
