Editorial

With Dr. V’s birth centenary getting closer, all the centres of Aravind are vibrant with several activities to commemorate the unique occasion and to pay tribute to the noble soul. Several programmes have been planned, many already underway to celebrate this occasion in the most meaningful way.

The success of the vision centre model has attracted several eye care providers and governments. Aravind provides guidance and support to set up vision centres in their regions. As part of this, Aravind has been working with the Govt. of Bangladesh towards setting up similar centres all over the country. A team from Aravind visited National Institute of Ophthalmology and gave all the necessary support in establishing vision centres, which will soon be opened for the benefit of a large number of people.

Aravind’s resident, Dr. K V Vaishali came out in flying colours at the recently held MS Ophthal university examinations receiving two gold medals.

News about these and many more feature in this issue.
